Frontpage Digitals (Rebrand to: GoGorilla)

At Frontpage Digital (now rebranded as GoGorilla), I initially joined a team dedicated to creating low-fidelity designs for a project called Portify. We leveraged Balsamiq to produce well-structured, user-centered wireframes. Later, I transitioned to the high-fidelity design team, where I contributed to the prototyping team to help bring the GoGorilla website to completion. Our primary focus was to design seamless, user-friendly animations and prototypes that elevated the user experience. Utilizing the capabilities of Figma and Framer for design, prototyping, and web development, our team successfully launched the GoGorilla website with efficiency and precision.
